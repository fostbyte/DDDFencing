Reliable Chain Link Fencing

Chain link fencing offers an excellent balance of affordability, durability, and security. Perfect for a wide range of applications, from residential pet containment to industrial perimeter security, our chain link solutions provide lasting value and peace of mind.

Perfect Applications:

Residential

Backyard enclosures, pet runs, and property boundaries

Commercial

Business perimeters, parking lots, and equipment areas

Industrial

Manufacturing facilities, storage yards, and utility sites

Recreational

Sports fields, playgrounds, and recreational facilities

Material Options & Specifications:

Galvanized Steel

  • Hot-dip galvanized coating
  • Corrosion resistant
  • 20+ year lifespan
  • Standard silver finish

Vinyl Coated

  • Color options available
  • Enhanced weather protection
  • Smooth, safe surface
  • Extended durability

Mesh Sizes

  • 2" diamond mesh (standard)
  • 1.5" diamond mesh (security)
  • Various wire gauges
  • Custom configurations

Height Options

  • 3 feet residential
  • 4-6 feet standard
  • 8-12 feet commercial
  • Custom heights available

Enhancement Options:

Privacy Slats

Vinyl slats in various colors for privacy and wind reduction.

Barbed Wire

Three-strand barbed wire topping for enhanced security.

Access Gates

Walk gates, drive gates, and automated gate systems.

Security Hardware

Locks, latches, and access control systems.

Chain Link Advantages:

Cost Effective

Most economical fencing option with excellent value for investment.

Low Maintenance

Minimal upkeep required - no painting, staining, or regular treatments.

Clear Visibility

See-through design maintains visual surveillance and openness.

Quick Installation

Faster installation compared to other fencing types.
